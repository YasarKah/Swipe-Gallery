### Description
`Smart Swipe helps you clean your photo library faster with a swipe-first experience designed for large, messy galleries.

Instead of getting lost in thousands of photos, Smart Swipe gives you focused cleanup flows that help you decide quickly and confidently. Swipe to keep, swipe to delete, review everything before removal, and make progress without feeling overwhelmed.

Key features:

- Swipe to Keep or Delete
Move through your photos quickly with a simple, satisfying swipe flow built for fast decisions.

- Guided Cleanup
Clean step by step, starting from the most recent months and moving into older years in a structured way.

- Similar Photos
Find repeated shots and trim down duplicate moments faster.

- Screenshots and Blurry Photos
Review the photos most likely to be clutter and free up storage more efficiently.

- Review Before Deleting
Nothing feels reckless. Check your delete queue before anything is removed.

- Month-by-Month Cleaning
Break large libraries into manageable groups so cleanup feels easier and more motivating.

- Progress That Stays With You
Resume where you left off and keep your cleanup sessions lightweight and consistent.

- Privacy First
Your photo decisions stay on your device. Your library is not uploaded for cleanup.

Smart Swipe is built for people who want a cleaner gallery without turning photo management into hard work.`
