Privacy Policy

Smart Swipe: Photo Cleaner accesses your photo library only to let you review media and decide what to keep or delete. Processing happens locally on your device.

Information We Access

The app requests photo library permission so it can display your photos and videos, organize them into cleanup groups, and move items you select into Apple’s system delete flow.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information, create accounts, run ads, or use third-party analytics SDKs.

How Your Data Is Used

Your media stays on your device. The app uses local storage to remember cleanup progress and preferences such as language selection or video inclusion.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
