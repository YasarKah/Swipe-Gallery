# Privacy Policy for Smart Swipe

Last updated: March 2026

Smart Swipe ("the App") is designed to help users review and clean up their photo library directly on their device.

## Information We Access

The App may request access to:

- your photo library

This access is used only to let you browse, group, review, keep, and delete photos or videos that you choose to manage inside the App.

## Information We Collect

At this time, the App does not collect personal data from users and does not upload photos or videos to external servers.

The App currently stores limited progress data on-device, such as:

- completed cleanup groups
- review progress within groups
- app preferences like selected language and settings

This data is stored locally on your device to support the user experience.

## Photo and Video Processing

Photo and video processing is performed locally on-device using Apple system frameworks. Your media is not transferred to our servers for analysis or cleanup.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

## Analytics and Tracking

The App does not currently use third-party analytics, ad tracking, or cross-app tracking.

If analytics, crash reporting, subscriptions, or other third-party services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

## Data Retention

Because the App currently stores only limited local progress and preference data on your device, you can remove that data by deleting the App from your device.

Photos and videos remain managed by Apple Photos and your iCloud/Photos library settings.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

You can:

- deny photo library access in iOS Settings
- revoke photo library access at any time in iOS Settings
- delete the App to remove locally stored app data

## Security

We aim to keep the App privacy-friendly by processing photo cleanup actions locally on-device whenever possible.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
